(2)gama 函数
----------------------------------------
Gama(alpha)=积分 0 到+无穷大 x^(alpha-1) * e^(-x) dx
性质:
Gama(alpha+1)=alpha*Gama(alpha);
Gama(n+1)=n!
Gama(1/2)=根号 Pi

常用：积分0到+无穷大 e^(-x) dx= 积分0到+无穷大 x^0 * e^(-x) dx= Gama(1)=0!=1
